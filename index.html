Research Interests

My primary interests involve developing robust sequential decision-making systems, particularly in settings where traditional assumptions are violated. E.g., when many actions must be taken simultaneously, there is distributional drift, and the feedback is corrupted or delayed.

I'm also interested in algorithm design and analysis more broadly, having previously done some work in graph theory, combinatorial optimization, and stochastic reaction networks.
